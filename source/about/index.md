## Welcome to the Realm of Negentropy

I'm KUN, a student at Zhejiang University's College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering. This website, **KUN's Tech Realm**, serves as my digital space for exploring the Un

## The Philosophy

The subtitle **"Realm of Negentropy"** reflects my approach to technology: creating order, structure, and information in a universe that tends toward chaos. Negentropy represents the measure of order, organization, and information - the opposite of entropy. Through technology, we can build islands of organization and meaning.

## My Mission

My guiding principle is: **"Attempting to reinterpret the world in the language of neurons."** I believe that understanding information processing can provide powerful insights for building better technological systems and solving complex problems.
